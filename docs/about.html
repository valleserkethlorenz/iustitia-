About Us

Discover the story behind Iustitia and our commitment to justice for all Filipinos.

Our Story

As Filipino citizens, we constantly witness the daily struggles of our countrymen, particularly how they face injustice without knowing what to do. This led us to a critical realization: many Filipinos are ignorant of Philippine Law and often lack access to legal help, making them extremely vulnerable to exploitation. This significant problem is just the tip of the iceberg. Driven by a desire to make an impact, we—a group of students—created "Iustitia" the Latin word for "justice", it is an innovative solution designed to address these legal challenges.

Our Vision

A society where every Filipino, regardless of circumstance, stands legally confident and protected, finally possessing the unrestricted access to justice they deserve.

Our Mission

To extinguish the vulnerability of every Filipino citizen to injustice by breaking down the barriers of complexity and cost that isolate them from their fundamental legal rights.
